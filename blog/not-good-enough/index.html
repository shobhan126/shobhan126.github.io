not good enough

“But if you really wanna do it right you have to consider that your rotation axis is slightly off centered and when you drive that gyroscope that slight variation will cause a wobble and…In theory you can have it work perfectly if…”

Some projects require a lot of care and precision. It becomes rather futile to start something if you know your methods are not good enough. So why waste your time?

This makes sense if it is some experiment requiring a lot of initial investment. Unfortunately I sometimes end up generalizing this mindset to things it clearly doesn’t apply to.

This website for instance - what a poorly written blog post this is! Grammar errors spilling around here and there; really boring prose. Not good enough. If I really wanna do it write, I should take some time (tending to infinity) to come up with more interesting content… In theory you can build the perfect website if you…

I guess I grew out of that faulty line of reasoning.
